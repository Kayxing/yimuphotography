Flying Unseen:

Sansui and Mia are two lesbians from China who live in New York and Los Angeles, respectively. They come from vastly different family and upbringing backgrounds, but both have traveled to the United States to build families that follow their own hearts.

They are like birds in the shadows, constantly flying towards freedom but not seen. Struggles with self-identity, familial constraints, expectations for the future, and confusion surround their lives, yet they are also the nutrients that contribute to their growth.

Through the lens, we break stereotypes, raise awareness, and build a safe zone for the community. Support Us!
